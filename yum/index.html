Lemon Chess Tarts

Each little tart delivers a bright lemony taste that cannot cannot be beaten. This quick recipe can be adjusted to fit any palate.

  • 1 1/3 cups sugar
  • 6 to 8 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Rind of two lemons grated
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 6 eggs well beaten
  • pastry Crust mix for 12 tart shells

Prepare and bake 12 tart shells. Mix sugar juice, sugar and rind

Melt butter on top of double boiler. Add sugar mixture and eggs.

Continue cooking over hot water until very thick, stir consistently

Cool cover and refridgerate. When chilled fill tart shells.
