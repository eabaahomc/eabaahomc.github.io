Lemon Chess Tarts

Ingredients

Each little tart delivers a bright lemony taste that cannot be beaten. This quick recipe can be adjusted to fit any palate.

  • 1 1/3 cups sugar
  • 6 to 8 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Rind of two lemons grated
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 6 eggs well beaten
  • pastry Crust mix for 12 tart shells

Direction

  1. Prepare and bake 12 tart shells. Mix sugar juice, sugar and rind
  2. Melt butter on top of double boiler. Add sugar mixture and eggs.
  3. Continue cooking over hot water until very thick, stir consistently
  4. Cool cover and refridgerate. When chilled fill tart shells.
